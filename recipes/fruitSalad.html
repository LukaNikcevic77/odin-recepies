Fruit salad recipe:

Make perfect fruit salad at home with this classic homemade fruit salad recipe, including a fruit salad dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Apple
  • 2 Oranges
  • 1 Graphefruit

Making steps:

  1. Wash all the fruits
  2. Chop all the fruits
  3. Mix them in a bowl
  4. Relax and enjoy
