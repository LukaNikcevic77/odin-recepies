Pizza recipe:

Make perfect pizza at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2cups of warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Making steps:

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Prepheat the pizza stone
  5. Divide teh dough into 2 balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten teh dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush teh dough top with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and Sprinkle with toppings
  11. Slide pizza into the oven
  12. Bake until its baked enough for your own taste
